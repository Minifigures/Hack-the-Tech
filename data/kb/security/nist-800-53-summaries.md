# NIST SP 800-53 Control Summaries

## ac-2

AC-2 (Account Management) requires the organisation to:

- Define and document the types of system accounts allowed and prohibited.
- Assign account managers for each account type.
- Require approval from designated personnel before creating an account.
- Establish conditions for group and role membership.
- Specify authorised users, group/role membership, and access authorisations
  for each account.
- Monitor account usage and notify account managers when accounts are no
  longer required, when users are terminated or transferred, or when
  individual usage changes.
- Authorise access to the system based on a valid access authorisation,
  intended system usage, and other attributes as defined.
- Review accounts on an organisation-defined frequency for compliance with
  account management requirements.

## ac-6

AC-6 (Least Privilege) requires the organisation to employ the principle of
least privilege, allowing only authorised accesses for users which are
necessary to accomplish assigned organisational tasks.

## ir-4

IR-4 (Incident Handling) requires the organisation to implement an incident
handling capability for security incidents that includes preparation,
detection and analysis, containment, eradication, and recovery; coordinates
incident handling activities with contingency planning; and incorporates
lessons learned into procedures and training.

## si-4

SI-4 (System Monitoring) requires monitoring the system to detect attacks and
indicators of potential attacks, unauthorised local, network, and remote
connections; and to deploy monitoring devices at strategic locations within
the system.
